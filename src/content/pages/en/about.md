I created this site to share everything I learn as I make new discoveries.

At heart, I am a maker: I need to tinker, test for myself, and build things to understand how they work under the hood. For me, tech isn't limited to a single specialty. It's by getting my hands dirty, building my own environments, and documenting my projects that I progress every day.

The Cloud Native and Kubernetes ecosystem is constantly evolving, and this portfolio is my public logbook. This is where I post my feedback, architectures, and research phases, with the idea that it can also help other enthusiasts.

If you like automation, technical curiosity, and learning by doing, you're in the right place. Enjoy your visit!
